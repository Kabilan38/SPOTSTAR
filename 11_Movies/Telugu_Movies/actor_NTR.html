About NTR Jr

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (born 20 May 1983), popularly known as NTR Jr, is an Indian actor, producer, and television presenter who primarily works in Telugu cinema.He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2012.Referred to in the media as the Man of Masses, he has starred in over 30 films.He is a recipient of several accolades including, three Filmfare Awards South, three CineMAA Awards, a SIIMA Award, a IIFA Award and two Nandi Awards.
