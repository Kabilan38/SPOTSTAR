About S.Janaki

S.Janaki (born April 23, 1938) is a legendary Indian playback singer, nicknamed the "Nightingale of South India," celebrated for her exceptionally versatile voice and prolific career spanning over six decades, recording thousands of songs in numerous languages, including Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada, becoming a beloved figure in South Indian cinema. She has won numerous accolades, including National and State Film Awards, and is known for hits like "Mizhiyoram" and "Thumbi Va Thumbakkudathin," although she briefly retired in 2017 before reversing the decision for select projects.
