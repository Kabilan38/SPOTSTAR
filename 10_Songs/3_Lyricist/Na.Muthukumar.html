About Na.Muthukumar

Na.Muthukumar (1975–2016) was a highly acclaimed Tamil film lyricist, poet, and author who penned over 1,500 songs in a career spanning from 1999 to 2016. He won two National Film Awards for Best Lyrics (Thanga Meenkal, Saivam) and holds the record for the most Filmfare Awards for Best Lyricist in Tamil.
