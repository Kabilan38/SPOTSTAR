About SPB

S.P.Balasubrahmanyam (SPB or Balu) (1946-2020) was a legendary Indian playback singer, actor, music director, and producer, famous for recording over 40,000 songs in 16 languages, including Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, and Hindi, holding a Guinness World Record for most songs and receiving India's Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan awards. Known as a versatile voice, he debuted in 1966 and became an iconic figure in Indian cinema before his passing from COVID-19 complications in 2020.
