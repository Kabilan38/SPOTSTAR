Aavesham - 2024

About the movie

Action
Comedy

Fahadh Faasil in a pivotal role as Ranga, a charismatic yet eccentric gangster. The story follows three college freshmen—Aju, Bibi, and Shanthan—who move from Kerala to Bangalore to pursue aeronautical engineering. They choose the BK Hostel for its reputation of freedom, but soon face brutal ragging from a senior student named Kutty and his gang, who abduct and severely beat them.
