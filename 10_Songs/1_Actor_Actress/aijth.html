About Aijth Kumar

Ajith Kumar (born May 1, 1971) is a renowned Indian actor and professional motor racer, primarily active in Tamil cinema for over three decades. Known as "AK" (formerly "Thala"), he has starred in over 60 films, winning multiple awards including Filmfare Awards South and a 2025 Padma Bhushan. He is married to former actress Shalini and has two children, Anoushka and Aadvik.
