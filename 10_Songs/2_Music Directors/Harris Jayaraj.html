About Harris Jayaraj

Harris Jayaraj is a highly acclaimed Indian film composer, primarily for Tamil cinema, known for his melodic and contemporary soundtracks in movies like Minnale, Ghajini, and Kaakha Kaakha, blending Carnatic roots with modern sounds, and has won numerous awards, including Filmfare South and Tamil Nadu State Awards.
