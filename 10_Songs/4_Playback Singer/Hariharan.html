About Hariharan

Hariharan is a legendary Indian playback singer and ghazal artist, known for his velvety voice, fusion music pioneering with Colonial Cousins, and versatility across Hindi, Tamil, and other languages, earning him the Padma Shri and National Awards. He comes from a musical family, mentored by his Carnatic musician parents, and rose to fame with hits like "Tu Hi Re" and "Roja Jaaneman," becoming a respected figure in Indian music.
