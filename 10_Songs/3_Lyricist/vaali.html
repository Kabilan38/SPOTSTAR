About Vaali

Vaali (1931–2013), born T. S. Rangarajan, was a legendary Tamil film lyricist and poet with over 15,000 songs spanning five decades, rivaling Kannadasan. Known for his work in the 1960s-70s MGR era and later with Ilaiyaraaja, he penned hits for MGR, Sivaji Ganesan, Kamal Haasan, and Rajinikanth. He was also an author, actor, and Padma Shri recipient.
