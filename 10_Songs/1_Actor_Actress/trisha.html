About Trisha Krishnan

Trisha Krishnan (born 4 May 1983) is a prominent Indian actress and model, primarily renowned for her work in Tamil and Telugu cinema over a career spanning more than two decades. Winning Miss Chennai in 1999, she rose to fame as a top actress in the 2000s, known for films like Ghilli (2004), Varsham (2004), 96 (2018), and Ponniyin Selvan. She is considered one of the highest-paid actresses in South India.
