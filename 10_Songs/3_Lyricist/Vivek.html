About Vivek

Vivek Velmurugan known by his stage name Vivek is an Indian lyricist working on Tamil language films. After making his debut in Enakkul Oruvan, he received critical acclaim for his work in 36 Vayadhinile, Jil Jung Juk, Mersal, Sarkar, Petta, Bigil and Jagame Thandhiram.
