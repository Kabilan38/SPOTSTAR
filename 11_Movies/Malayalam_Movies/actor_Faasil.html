About Fahadh Faasil

Abdul Hameed Mohammed Fahadh Fazil, professionally known as Fahadh Faasil or FaFaa, is an Indian actor and producer who predominantly works in Malayalam and Tamil films. Noted for his diverse portrayals, Fahadh is among the highest-paid Malayalam actors.
