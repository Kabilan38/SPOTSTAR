About Karthik

Singer Karthik (born November 7, 1980) is a prominent Indian playback singer and composer, known for his versatile voice in over 8,000 songs across 15+ Indian languages, including Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi, starting as a backing vocalist for A.R. Rahman and becoming a popular voice in South Indian cinema with hits like "Ale Ale" and "Oru Maalai".
