About Samantha

Samantha Ruth Prabhu (born April 28, 1987) is a leading Indian actress and producer known for her work in Tamil and Telugu cinema. Debuting in 2010 with Ye Maaya Chesave, she has become one of South India's highest-paid actresses. She is celebrated for her versatility, winning multiple Filmfare Awards, and is known for her resilience, often overcoming personal health struggles.
