About Yuvan

Yuvan Shankar Raja (born Aug 31, 1979) is a versatile Indian film composer, singer, and songwriter, primarily for Tamil cinema, known as the "Youth Icon of Tamil Film Music" for introducing hip-hop and remixes, youngest son of Ilaiyaraaja, and an award-winning artist for hits like 7G Rainbow Colony, Mounam Pesiyathey, and Raam, making his debut at 16 and becoming a major force with over 100 films.
