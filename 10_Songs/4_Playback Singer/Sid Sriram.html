About Sid Sriram

Sid Sriram (born May 19, 1990) is a renowned Indian-American playback singer, music producer, and songwriter known for blending Carnatic music with R&B, soul, and pop. Based in Chennai and California, he rose to fame with "Adiye" (2013) and is a prominent voice in South Indian cinema.
