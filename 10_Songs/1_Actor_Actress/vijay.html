About Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974), known as Vijay or "Thalapathy," is a premier Indian actor, playback singer, and politician in Tamil cinema. With a 30-year career and ~69 films, he is among India's highest-paid actors. In 2024, he launched the political party Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) to contest the 2026 elections.
