About the movie

Madhavan Nair’s accidental killing of a rival family's teenager leads his son Raja to take the fall, resulting in a five-year prison sentence and estrangement from his father. After his release, Raja reinvents himself as "Pokkiri Raja," becoming a key figure in a gangster's operations in Madurai.
