About Nazriya

Nazriya Nazim Fahadh is an Indian actress and film producer who works predominantly in Malayalam and Tamil films. Nazriya is a recipient of several accolades including two Kerala State Film Awards, one Tamil Nadu State Film Award and one Filmfare Award South. Nazriya started her career as an presenter in 2005.
