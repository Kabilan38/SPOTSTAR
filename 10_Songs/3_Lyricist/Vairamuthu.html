About Vairamuthu

Vairamuthu Ramaswamy (born 1953) is a highly acclaimed Indian poet, lyricist, and author known for his40+ year career in the Tamil film industry. Debuting in 1980 with Nizhalgal, he has written over 7,500 songs, winning seven National Film Awards and honors including the Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan, and Sahitya Akademi Award.
