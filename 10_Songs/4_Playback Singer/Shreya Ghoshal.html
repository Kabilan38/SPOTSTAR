About Shreya Ghoshal

Shreya Ghoshal (born March 12, 1984) is a highly acclaimed Indian playback singer, celebrated for her versatile voice and vast repertoire across many languages, launching her career after winning Sa Re Ga Ma Pa and debuting in Devdas (2002). Known for hits like " Piyu Bole" and " Deewani Mastani," she has won numerous awards, including National Film Awards, and is recognized for her dedication to classical training, becoming a leading figure in Indian cinema, married to Shiladitya Mukhopadhyaya with a son, Devyaan, and even having a wax figure at Madame Tussauds.
