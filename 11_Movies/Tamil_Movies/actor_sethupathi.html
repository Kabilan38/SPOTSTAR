About Vijay Sethupathi

Vijay Gurunatha Sethupathi Kalimuthu credited as Vijay Sethupathi is an Indian actor and film producer who predominantly works in Tamil films. He has also worked in Telugu, Malayalam and Hindi Films. He is a recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award, two Filmfare Awards South and two Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.
