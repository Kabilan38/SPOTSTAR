Mankatha - 2011

About the movie

Action
Thriller
Crime

In "Mankatha," suspended cop Vinayak Mahadevan teams up with a gang to steal ₹500 crore meant for cricket betting but faces betrayal and a high-stakes chase as both the police and local goons close in. The story escalates into a battle between Vinayak and tough cop Prithvi as the cash goes missing.
