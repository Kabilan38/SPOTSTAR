About A.R.Rahman

A.R. Rahman (born A.S. Dileep Kumar on January 6, 1967) is a globally acclaimed Indian composer, singer, and songwriter known for revolutionizing Indian cinema music by blending classical styles with electronic music. As of early 2026, he is 59 years old, having built a legendary career from his debut in Mani Ratnam’s Roja (1992) to winning two Academy Awards for Slumdog Millionaire.
