About Sivakarthikeyan

Sivakarthikeyan (born February 17, 1985) is a popular Indian actor, producer, singer, and television personality known for his versatile roles in Tamil cinema, rising from a stand-up comedian and TV host on Vijay TV to a successful film star in family entertainers like Varuthapadatha Valibar Sangam and Doctor, also producing films through his banner Sivakarthikeyan Productions. He's celebrated for his steady rise, family-friendly appeal, and expanding career as a singer and lyricist, winning several awards, including Vijay Awards.
