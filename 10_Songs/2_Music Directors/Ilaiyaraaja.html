About Ilaiyaraaja

Ilaiyaraaja (born R. Gnanathesigan, 1943) is a legendary Indian film composer, singer, and songwriter, widely regarded as a musical genius (Isaignani) who revolutionized South Indian cinema. Debuting in 1976 with Annakili, he has composed over 6,000 songs and provided scores for 1,000+ films in Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, and Hindi.
