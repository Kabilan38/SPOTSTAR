About G.V.Prakash

G.V. Prakash Kumar is a prominent Indian music composer, actor, and singer, primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born on June 13, 1987, into a musically inclined family (nephew of A.R. Rahman), he debuted as a composer with Veyil (2006) and gained massive acclaim for his work on Aadukalam. He transitioned to acting in Darling (2015) and has since featured in films like Sarvam Thaala Mayam.
