About Nayanthara

Nayanthara (born Diana Mariam Kurien on November 18, 1984) is a highly acclaimed Indian actress and producer, widely known as the "Lady Superstar" of South Indian cinema. With a career spanning over two decades, she has appeared in over 75 films, primarily in Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam, earning five Filmfare Awards South and becoming one of the highest-paid actresses.
