About Anirudh

Anirudh Ravichander (born 16 October 1990) is a renowned Indian music composer, singer, and producer, known for redefining South Indian film music with hits like "Why This Kolaveri Di" (2012). Born to actor Ravi Raghavendra and dancer Lakshmi, he debuted at age 21, quickly becoming a top composer in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi cinema with credits like Vikram, Jawan, and Leo.
